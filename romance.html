As with almost any travel decision, the best cruise is always the cruise that is best for you. Would you prefer to sail up Norway's fjords or to play the turquoise waters of the Mediterranean? Is your family interested in a themed cruise frequented by Disney characters, or do you and your partner want an intimate, romantic river trip? Would you rather pack a tux or a sturdy pair of hiking books? With an expanding variety of journeys as well as destinations, even the skeptical are likely to find something here that will pique their interest.


Top 5 Dinners

Secret Cave Dining

Experience a very unique and romantic dinner at the Samabe Bali Suites & Villas. Imagine your own secret hideaway in a cave next to the ocean. Enjoy a five course special romantic menu, with your own dedicated chef and staff, as you are serenaded by the crashing waves.

Learn More

Pontoon Dining

During your stay at the fabulous Turtle Island in Fiji, your personal concierge will arrange a private dinner on the floating pontoon in the famous Blue Lagoon. Your boatman will deliver you by candle light to the pontoon, where you’ll be served an intimate dinner accompanied by a bottle of bubbly.

Learn More

Treehouse Dining

On a secure and lavish platform where the stars are impossible to count, this treehouse has all the luxurious comforts, but begets an experience as wild as the animals that roam beneath it at Lion Sands Game Preserve. You’ll be escorted to the treehouse at sunset, where drinks and a scrumptious picnic dinner await.

Learn More

Sand Castle Dining

Your dinner on Velassaru Island in the Maldives will be a truly memorable experience. Your private banquet is dug into the sugary sands of the South Male Atoll. Romantic candles and torches set the mood for romance.

Learn More

Amalfi Coast Dining

At dinner, Michelin starred La Sponda restaurant in Positano at Le Sirenuse is lit by four hundred candles that create an unforgettable atmosphere. Your view of the Amalfi Coast will be spectacular. That, combined with the award winning Mediterranean cuisine will create a memory you’ll cherish for a lifetime.

Top 5 Resorts

Ladera

This island retreat stands out for its open-walled rooms — all the better for taking in panoramic vistas of the stunning Caribbean scenery — which still feel private, thanks to ’s mountainside location.

Belmond Castello di Casole

Spread across 4,200 acres of Tuscan countryside, centers around a 10th-century castle, carefully restored to highlight antique touches, including wood-beam ceilings and Etruscan artifacts found on-site. Among the more unique activities for outdoor enthusiasts: on-site truffle hunting.

Learn More

Amandari

Though not far from Ubud’s city center, the luxe transports guests light years away from civilization with its perch on the hills above the Ayung River gorge. Suites mimic local village houses in their design and provide total seclusion for peace-seeking couples who want to enjoy traditional Balinese culture.

Surf & Sand

The Mediterranean-leaning in Laguna Beach captures the laid-back elegance for which the Orange County town is famed. The oceanfront pool and adjacency to Bluebird Beach make it popular among couples who want quick access to water sports.

Calistoga Ranch, an Auberge

Nature enthusiasts flock to this idyllic hideaway, where 50 rustic luxury cottages dot 157 acres filled with oak trees and trickling streams. Couples can find rejuvenation at however they define it, whether that’s zip-lining through redwoods, biking through vineyards, or practicing alfresco yoga.

Top 5 Hikes

Krn Mountain

For those adventurous types searching for a place no friend of theirs has experienced is quite hard, but that's where Slovenia comes in. This unexplored Krn Mountain is located in the Julian Alps and one of its peaks hits more than 7,000 feet. It is a place that needs to be visited as it is so romantic and magical.

Learn More

The Queen Charlotte Track

There are so many different places on those sixteen acres of vineyards and gardens at The Marlborough Lodge that you can romantically wander together, but what makes it so special is the excursion to the Queen Charlotte Track. For safety reasons, it is guided, but there you will be able to hike around the magical forest with native ferns and foliage all while learning about Maori history.

Learn More

Arkaba Walk

If you are someone who likes to take cool romantic pictures of their hikes, one of the most Instagrammable places you can go to is the Arkaba Walk. It is just a couple of hours from Adelaide and it is full of gorges, wild animals as well as cool Aboriginal rock art. A popular thing to do there is sleeping under the stars and waking up to a magical sunrise.

Learn More

Koko Crater Trail

Rather than going on a popular hike that everyone chooses to do, choose the Koko Crater Trail that is also very close to Waikiki Beach. It is great for advanced hikes, and there will be a romantic view waiting for you on the top of an extinct volcano.

Learn More

Fira to Oia

Even though everyone knows that Santorini is one of the top romantic places you can go, it is simply with it. It has some of the best hikes like the one from Fira to Oia. It is an approximately six-mile hike where you will be able to experience an amazing sunset viewpoint, reinvigorating swimming pools as well as amazing historical sites along the way.
